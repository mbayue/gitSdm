## 2023-10-27 - Icon-only buttons accessibility pattern
**Learning:** Icon-only buttons (like the Sparkles button in the Navbar) need explicit `aria-label` and `title` attributes to be accessible to screen readers and provide hover context for sighted users.
**Action:** When adding or reviewing icon-only buttons, ensure they always include `aria-label` (for screen readers) and `title` (for tooltips on hover).
